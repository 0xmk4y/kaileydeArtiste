I’m Kailey. I live in Accra - Ghana, where I design the future.

Kailey is a versatile and gifted artist who is skilled in makeup,costume design, and creative directions. Her passion for her work will bring out the true inner depth of beauty in what you vision. ABOUT ME From sfx makeup direction to creative direction and costume -making, Kailey has proven there’s nothing she can’t beat in her field. From a young age she has believed, “You can create anything once you see it in your mind”.

As a makeup artist,Kailey’s client sheet consists of socialites like Coco Benson,Abiana Dickson,Kuami Eugene and many others. Kailey’s mission is to connect women of all color and to pave way for women of color to express their feelings through art.

She created “Afriartz” a modeling platform to link women all over Africa to portray art in different concepts. From creating concepts and costume designing for well known Afrobeats artists like Stonebwoy, Shatta Wale,Medikal and more. “Styled by Kailey” emerged from her creative costume direction and venture.Kailey’s costume has been worned by stars both in the music and movie industry.
